iOS (iPhone and iPad): You can install the app from the Apple App Store. Simply open the App Store, search for the “Style Cycle” app , and tap the "Install" button. The app will be downloaded and installed on your device's home screen. Android: Apps for Android devices can be installed from the Google Play Store. Open the Play Store app, search for the “Style Cycle” app, and tap the "Install" button. The app will be installed on your device.
