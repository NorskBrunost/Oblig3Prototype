StyleCycle

Mission statement

Our goal is to promote a culture of reuse, enabling people to give their clothes a new life, every wardrobe becomes a source of creativity, and together, we frame a more sustainable world.

One manifestation of our mission

Our company provides an app that can make outfits for you based on the clothes you already have in your closet. The app uses artificial intelligence to suggest different outfits to you based on the clothes you have, the weather, the event or a dress code.

Challenges we subsequently wish to approach

According to Dzhengiz et al. (2023) the fashion industry is a major contributor to the world´s climate pollution and is polluting almost as much as the world´s oil extraction and production. While many of us have closets full of clothes, we still succumb to the temptation to buy new clothes. With the rise of fast fashion (which are affordable clothes produced in a rapid speed) many of the clothes we buy are never worn until we forget them or throw them away. (World Bank, 2019 & Dzhengiz et.al. 2023)
We at StyleCycle believe that if more people know how to style the clothes they already own, we can be on our way to a more sustainable future.

Problems we want to tackle:

  • Help people reduce their costs on buying new clothes.
  • Make people reuse their clothes in regard to the environment.
  • Help people to make an outfit out of existing clothes in their wardrobe, by using a built in AI-helper.
